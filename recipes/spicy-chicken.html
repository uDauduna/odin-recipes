Spicy Chicken

Description

Spicy Fried Chicken is a crispy and juicy treat with a bold kick of heat, coated in a flavorful spice blend and fried to perfection. Perfect for gatherings or as a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken in buttermilk for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour with cayenne pepper, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan. Dredge the chicken pieces in the flour mixture, ensuring an even coat. Fry until golden brown and fully cooked, about 10-15 minutes per piece. Drain on paper towels before serving.
